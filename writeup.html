Best Restaurant Near Me - Connective Information


Background

In this project, we want to help customers explore the best restaurants near them. To achieve this goal, we obtained the "yelp_academic_dataset_business" dataset from Yelp's official website, explore the dataset to uncover valuable information, and clean the dataset to maximize the visualizable data. Besides, we created an interactive map and a dashboard, including six visualizations, each showing the restaurants' performance, attributes, and distribution in the United State separately.

Interactive map

We created an interactive United State map as a connection to all the visuals. By selecting a state on the US map, the dashboard will present the six visualizations of restaurants located in that state.

Interactive map

Within the dashboard, we designed an interactive plot with drop-down options for users to select the day of the week they are interested in and review the relationships between the restaurant's business hours and its rating star on that day.

Contextual visualizations

To present more information about the restaurants, we built a dashboard with five visualizations. The first three charts demonstrate the restaurants' attributes. The dataset stores boolean representing whether the restaurant has parking, is animal friendly, or has Wi-Fi. To visualize the boolean information, we built three bar charts to compare the number of true and false. Through the bar charts, the user will grasp the proportion of restaurants that have parking, are animal friendly, and have Wi-Fi. In addition, the scatter plot on the dashboard shows the relationship between the review count received by the restaurant and its rating-star. Besides, the dashboard also presents the map for the state, which reflects the distribution of the restaurants' locations in the state and categories of restaurants, including bars, foods and etc. The color of points on the map then represents the different categories.

In conclusion, through the dashboard, users can comprehend the overall performance of restaurants near them in an efficient and easier way.
